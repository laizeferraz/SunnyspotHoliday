Things to do and to see:

  • Visit the National Park.
  • Nearby Tennis courts and Bowling Club.
  • Watch the migrating whales between June and August.
  • Delight in stroling through the bush to see local birds and wildlife.
  • Excellent fishing.
  • Swin in the safety to the serene beach creek with the children. The creek is well suited to those who wish to learn to snorkel with its safe waters and abundant aquatic life.
  • Boating, sailing, and other water sports.
  • Or just relax in our friendly park and enjoy the tranquillity!

When you have the beach nearby there is always plenty to do. Being only a hop, step and jump from the creek, beach and bush, it's a nature-based holiday...a breath of fresh air.
