Looking for a relaxing holiday?

Spend quality time together with the sounds of the many local birds and the gentle surf. This property offers a unique coastal experience, catering to everyone's accommodation requirements.

Lie back and enjoy the holiday park's shady serenity in the midst of the paper bark trees that surround it. The holiday park faces the sea, with bright blue wateres that attract fishermen and water sports enthusiasts the whole year round.

Accommodation

There are 18 different cabins to choose from, from standard cabins, to the deluxe cabins only 50 metres from the beach

Camping

The openness and flat sites at Sunnyspot Holidays make this park a great choice for your holiday.

Activities

Being only a hop, step and a jump from the creek, beach and bush, it’s a nature based holiday … a breath of fresh air!

Climate

The climate is noted for being one of the best in Australia with average temperatures up to 32C in Summer and 21C in Winter

Location

Sunny Spot Holidays is located 5 hrs north of Sydney on the beautiful east coast of New South Wales

Contact

Our family looks forward to welcoming you to Sunnyspot Holidays, contact us for reservations, travel advice or just a chat.
